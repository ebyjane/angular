Why takeajob.com?

Takeajob is one of the successful and emerging online job portals. Our unique features and unmatched technology can assist you in finding the perfect fit in position. You will be provided with thousands of employers from different industries.

How to Register with takeajob.com?

To register with takeajob.com, Open the home page and click on job seeker registration then fill in the registration page with all details and submit it. By doing so you will receive your default password through an E-mail which you need to change it once you login.

How a Resume can be Post?

While registering with takeajob.com, you will be asked to fill in your details. Very next to that you will receive a welcome mail, following that it will lead to the page, where you have to upload your resume.

How a Profile can be Updated or Edited?

After logging in, under the home page you can find an option named edit profile. From there you can upload a new resume.

How to Recover a Forgotten Password?

At login page you can find a link for forget password. By entering the registered e-mail ID, a new password will be sent to the e-mail, which has to be changed by logging in.

What is Geo Tagging?

Making note of a specific terrestrial area by its longitude and latitude is geo tagging. Takeajob.com is unique in this kind to offer such an option to the users.
